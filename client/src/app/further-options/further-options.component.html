Dance Partners


This feature is pretty new in some cities. Please invite your friends to the Telegram Groups below.


You can add The Dance Planner to your Telegram Group. If your Telegram group contains a city name like e.g. Heidelberg, the Dance Planner will automatically send you infos about new events.




Recommendation

To use all features of the Dance Planner download the Brave Browser and Telegram
